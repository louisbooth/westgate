Markets
Join us each Thursday afternoon and Saturday morning for our community market and workday. Find a wide selection of organically grown produce and plants grown right here at the farm. Local vendors and artisans join the market on Saturdays, selling jams, honey, plants, baked goods, crafts and much more!

We accept cash and SNAP Benefits. All SNAP customers receive 50% off each purchase through the Fresh Access Bucks Program!

Thursday market: 2pm- 6pm
Saturday market: 9am- 1pm

Westgate Greenmarket Express
The Westgate Greenmarket Express, affectionately called Curby, brings fresh, locally grown produce directly to neighbors in the Westgate and Belvedere Homes community. Using an electric vehicle, this innovative mobile market drives a winding route every Saturday morning from November through May. We sell produce from the Westgate Community Farm and other local producers. The music playing from the cart lets people know the greenmarket is coming up the street, and has earned the market the nickname “the ice cream truck for vegetables”.

The Westgate Greenmarket Express started in the Fall of 2011, and is a project of the Westgate/Belvedere Homes CRA. The mobile greenmarket brings neighbors together as they buy produce, say hello, and swap recipes for familiar and new veggies. It also makes fresh produce more accessible to community members with limited mobility, due to transportation or physical limitations.

Find us in the neighborhood every Saturday from 9am – 2pm!

We accept cash and SNAP Benefits. All SNAP customers receive 50% off each purchase through the Fresh Access Bucks Program!
